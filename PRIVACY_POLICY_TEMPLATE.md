## 6. Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect it:

**Technical Safeguards:**
- Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Secure authentication (JWT tokens)
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and role-based permissions
- Secure password storage (hashed, never stored in plain text)

**Organizational Safeguards:**
- Staff training on privacy and security
- Confidentiality agreements for all staff
- Limited access to personal information (need-to-know basis)
- Regular review of security practices

**Physical Safeguards:**
- Secure server infrastructure
- Restricted access to physical records
- Secure disposal of records

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations:

**Medical Records:**
- **Adults:** 7 years after your last appointment or until you turn 25 (whichever is longer)
- **Children (under 18):** Until you turn 25 years old
- **Legal requirements:** Some records may be retained longer if required by law

**Financial Records:**
- 7 years (as required by Australian tax law)

**Account Information:**
- While your account is active
- 7 years after account closure (for legal and compliance purposes)

When information is no longer needed, we securely delete or de-identify it in accordance with our data retention policy.

## 8. Your Rights

Under the Australian Privacy Act 1988, you have the following rights:

### 8.1 Access (APP 12)

You have the right to access your personal information. You can:
- Request a copy of your personal information
- Request information in a specific format (JSON, PDF, CSV)
- Access your information through your account dashboard

**How to Request Access:**
- Log in to your account and use the "Request My Data" feature
- Contact us at [privacy@yourclinic.com.au]
- We will respond within 30 days

### 8.2 Correction (APP 13)

You have the right to correct inaccurate, incomplete, or out-of-date information. You can:
- Update your information through your account
- Contact us to request corrections
- We will respond within 30 days

### 8.3 Deletion (APP 13)

You have the right to request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal retention requirements. You can:
- Request deletion through your account
- Contact us at [privacy@yourclinic.com.au]

**Note:** We may be required to retain certain information for legal or compliance purposes (e.g., medical records for 7 years). If we cannot delete your information, we will explain why.

### 8.4 Withdraw Consent

You can withdraw your consent for certain uses of your information at any time:
- Marketing communications - use the unsubscribe link or contact us
- Data sharing - contact us to withdraw consent
- Telehealth services - you can opt-out at any time

**Note:** Withdrawing consent may affect our ability to provide certain services.

### 8.5 Complaints

If you believe we have breached the Australian Privacy Principles, you can:
1. Contact us at [privacy@yourclinic.com.au]
2. Make a complaint to the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC):
   - Website: [www.oaic.gov.au](https://www.oaic.gov.au)
   - Phone: 1300 363 992
   - Email: [enquiries@oaic.gov.au](mailto:enquiries@oaic.gov.au)
